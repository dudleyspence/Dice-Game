Welcome to the dice game! First choose how may dice you want to play with. You can play with 3-6 dice. Once you have chosen how many dice you wish to play with press the roll button to play your first round and roll the dice. The scoreboard will show you how many rounds you have had, how many points you have won that round and the total number of points you have earned. Below the scoreboard is a table of scores from the previous 5 rounds.

  • All the same: This will earn you 60 points plus the sum of the dice.
  • All but one the same: This will earn you 40 points plus the sum of the dice.
  • A straight: e.g. (2,3,4,5) This will earn you 20 points plus the sum of the dice.
  • All different: If all the dice are different and not a staight you will earn the sum of the dice in points.
  • Any other outcome: 0 points will be awarded
